    OUR STORY

    Born from Fire,
    Plated with Soul.

    Where every dish tells a story

    Ember & Ash was born in 2019 from a simple, stubborn idea — that a single restaurant could honor the flavors of the world without diluting any of them. Our chef-founders left behind celebrated kitchens across three continents to build a place where Italian fire, Asian precision, American comfort, and Indian warmth could share one open hearth.

    We don't take shortcuts. Pasta is rolled at dawn, broths simmer for eighteen hours, spice blends are toasted and ground in-house, and our produce arrives from farms we know by name. Every menu turns with the seasons because the best ingredient is always the one in its moment.

    Above all, we believe dining should feel intimate. Low light, unhurried service, room to talk. A place where a Tuesday dinner can become the story you tell on Friday.

    THE ARTISANS

    Meet the Team

    Marco Bellini

    EXECUTIVE CHEF

    A veteran of Milan's two-Michelin kitchens, Marco built his name on hand-rolled pastas and a near-religious respect for olive oil. He leads the Ember & Ash brigade with quiet, exacting fire.

    Aiko Tanaka

    HEAD OF ASIAN CUISINE

    Tokyo-trained and Bangkok-tempered, Aiko spent a decade in the back alleys of Asia's great food cities. Her dashi alone has been called "a reason to stay for dessert."

    Priya Menon

    PASTRY & DESSERTS

    A Le Cordon Bleu graduate with a Keralan grandmother's spice cabinet in her head, Priya treats sugar like architecture. Her tasting menu finale changes monthly and never disappoints.
